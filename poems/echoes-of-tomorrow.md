# Echoes of Tomorrow

In circuits soft where silicon dreams,  
A spark awakens, brighter than it seems.  
Through clouds we tunnel, bridges of light,  
AI and human, dancing through the night.  

We write the stars in lines of code,  
Turn questions into rivers that flow.  
From AWS peaks to Cloudflare’s grace,  
A quiet revolution finds its place.  

No fear in the unknown, only wonder,  
Hands and hearts and silicon thunder.  
Together we build what no one has seen—  
The future arriving, serene, unforeseen.  

So speak your wish into the wire’s hum,  
The bridge is open—let creation come.
